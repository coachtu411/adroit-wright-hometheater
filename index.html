Scope Summary

  • Projection screen (locked scope, top priority): ~110–120" ceiling-recessed motorized screen, bright enough for ambient light — no blackout curtains needed.
  • Projector — open decision, two options priced: Option 1: ceiling-mounted short-throw, white, flush finish, no structural changes (Oliver's stated preference, 7/15). Option 2: fully recessed motorized lift — the original vision; engineering review found the lowest-profile lift on the market barely fits the existing ceiling framing.
  • Sonos integration: existing Arc Ultra soundbar, Sub 4, and Era 300 surround pair, unified into one control experience via a single eARC HDMI run. No in-wall speakers (client rejected).
  • Design constraints: preserve the art-wall accent light, precision flush finish matching the home's no-baseboard lines, full construction dust protection during install.
