Reference for every term used in the estimate tool above, in the order they matter most to reading the numbers.
System Assurance & Warranty Package
An internal margin line (default 5% of the active raw subtotal), computed automatically and folded into the client-facing price. It is real margin, not a pass-through cost, and is never itemized or named as a fee to the client.
Raw subtotal
The sum of every active line item's qty × unit rate before the margin is applied — Adroit's standard cost for the job as currently toggled.
Line item / Line total
One row of billable work (equipment or labor). Line total = quantity × unit rate, computed automatically and not directly editable.
Ceiling-recessed motorized screen
A projection screen that retracts up into a pocket built into the ceiling when not in use, appearing flush/invisible — the locked-scope Section A item.
Short-throw projector
A projector designed to sit close to the screen (a few feet, not the back of the room) and still fill it — required here so the unit can be ceiling-mounted or lift-concealed near the screen wall rather than at the back of the room.
Projector lift (e.g. AeroLift-class)
A motorized mechanism that lowers a projector out of a ceiling recess for use and raises it back up out of sight afterward — Option 2's approach; requires more ceiling depth/tolerance than a fixed ceiling mount.
eARC (Enhanced Audio Return Channel)
An HDMI feature carrying high-quality audio back from the display/projector to a soundbar over a single cable — how the existing Sonos Arc Ultra will connect, avoiding separate audio wiring.
ADI Global Distribution
Adroit's AV/security equipment dealer — the source for actual (non-retail-list) screen and lift pricing; not yet quoted for this job.
Mobilization
Time and cost to get the crew, tools, and materials staged and the work area protected before productive install work begins — priced once per project, not per task.
Turnkey scope
A pricing structure where Adroit supplies both labor and materials (screen, projector, mounting/lift hardware, conduit, AV cabinet). The client's existing Sonos system is the exception — integrated, not replaced or supplied.
Fixed price
A single agreed total price for the defined scope of work, as opposed to time-and-materials billing.
Change order
A documented addition or modification to the original scope (and its price) after work has started.
Deposit / Balance
The upfront percentage of the total price collected to begin work (deposit), with the remainder (balance) due upon completion.
